Tips & Trik Jitu Rank Up di Game Android 2025

1. Optimasi Performa HP

  1. Masuk Developer Option → matikan animation scale.
  2. Gunakan Game Space → pilih performance mode.
  3. Update GPU driver lewat Play Store.

2. Settingan Touch Sampling & Gyro

  • Touch sampling minimal 360 Hz.
  • Gyro sensitivitas 220-240 untuk flick shot.

3. Jadwal Main & Mental Booster

Main di jam 07.00-09.00 (server paling sepi). Hindari late-night ranked saat weekend.

4. Latihan Aim & Map Awareness

  • Gunakan aim trainer (Aim Lab Mobile).
  • Tonton mini-map tiap 3 detik.

Contoh meta MLBB saat ini: Tank/Utility (Chip), Mage (Valentina), Gold Laner (Irithel), Jungler (Ling), Roamer (Kaja).
